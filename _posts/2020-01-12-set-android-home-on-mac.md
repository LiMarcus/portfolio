1. Default`$ export ANDROID_HOME=/Applications/ADT/sdk`
2. My mac: `$export ANDROID_HOME=/Users/feng/Library/Android/sdk `
3. Add this to the PATH environment variable: `$ export PATH=$PATH:$ANDROID_HOME/platform-tools`
